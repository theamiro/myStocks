About Eegads Ltd(EGAD)

Eaagads is engaged in growing, blending and selling of coffee products both locally and in the International market. It produces approximately 292 tons of coffee annually. The company sold some of its land to Tatu city, a property development project by Moscow-based Renaissance Partners. Renaissance is the largest shareholder in Eaagads, a stake it acquired after it bought out the majority shareholder Socfinaf.
